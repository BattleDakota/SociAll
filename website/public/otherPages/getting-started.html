How To Use SociAll

Step 1: Create an account!

Navigate to the home page using the link above and scroll down to log in or register a new account! SociAll will ask for some preliminary information including your email, desired username, as well as prompt you to create a password. Choose your username carefully, as this is how friends will find you!

Step 2: Update your info!

Once you have your account made, you're already almost there! Now just log in, choose a profile picture, and fill out any associated information that you want displayed to those who view your page. At the moment this includes Snapchat, Facebook, and Twitter, with more coming soon!

Step 3: All set!

Meet someone new and want to quickly and easily give them all of your various handles at once? Have them text your SociAll username to (385)236-0797!
